12:00 - 14:00

19:00 - 22:00
Glucose Disposal Walk: A 15-minute gentle walk post-dinner utilizes leg musculature to clear blood glucose, protecting the pancreas and aiding evening digestion.

Dusk Sequence Complete

Nutritional Alchemy

Lipid Optimization

Replace saturated animal fats (ghee, butter) with monounsaturated oils (olive, canola, peanut) for daily tadka. This preserves the HDL (good cholesterol) while actively lowering LDL markers.

Omega Fortification

Integrate 1 tablespoon of ground flaxseed into daily chapati dough. This introduces essential Omega-3 fatty acids for cardiovascular health and joint lubrication without altering traditional textures.

Grain Transition

Adults require 21 to 30 grams of fibre daily. Transition from refined white rice to brown rice (starting with a 50/50 mix) and incorporate oats upma to leverage soluble fibre for blood sugar stabilization.

Fructose Sourcing

Substitute processed sweets and fruit juices with whole fruits (baked apples, fresh orange segments). The intact cellular structure and pectin ensure natural sweetness without metabolic disruption.

Somatic Regulation

Breathwork
Nadi Shodhana (Alternate Nostril Breathing):
Balances the autonomic nervous system. Inhale through the left nostril (parasympathetic activation), exhale through the right. Repeat for 5 minutes to drastically lower blood pressure and cortisol before sleep.

Vital Metrics & Longevity

Annual Clinical Biomarkers

Maintenance
  • Bone Density Scan (DEXA)
    Critical evaluation for osteopenia. Hormonal shifts post-50 accelerate bone mass loss up to 15%. Early detection prevents skeletal fragility.
  • Metabolic Circumference
    Visceral adiposity (abdominal fat) is a primary driver of metabolic syndrome. Thresholds must remain below 40 inches (men) and 35 inches (women) to mitigate diabetes risk.
  • Endothelial Health
    Consistent blood pressure monitoring. Hypertension operates asymptomatically and is the leading vector for cardiovascular events.
  • Routine Oncology Screens
    Mandatory adherence to scheduled mammography, prostate, and colon screenings in the sixth decade of life.
